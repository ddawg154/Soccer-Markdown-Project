# Basic Soccer Positions
[← Back to Players](players.md)

Soccer has several major positions:
### Goalkeeper
The only player allowed to use their hands.
### Defenders
Players who protect the goal and stop attacks.
### Midfielders
Players who control the pace of the game and connect defense to attack.
### Forwards
Players who score goals.
---

## Small Position Table
| Position | Main Job |
|---------|----------|
| GK | Stop shots |
| DEF | Defend |
| MID | Control midfield |
| FWD | Score |
